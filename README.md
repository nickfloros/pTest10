### pTest10

Live wind speed and direction for few windsurding spots in South of England
- Highcliffe
- Portland
- Lyndhurst
- Hurst Castle.

Data is courtesy of http://www.r-p-r.co.uk/index.html

The app demonstrates use of 
- Polymer components
- Google Maps
- Google Charts
- Bootstract style
- Integration with Google App Enginee
